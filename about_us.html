Get to know us

Since the dawn of humanity, space has always held a particular allure for humans, especially as the sky presents itself as an embellished abstract canvas adorned with twinkling stars and planets that frame the space, rendering it a fascinating beauty.

The more humans gaze at the sky, the more they are captivated by space, a distant and mysterious world. Today, science enables humans to explore this space filled with mysteries, as well as truths yet undiscovered. The idea of establishing this club aligns with a vision of uncovering these truths through social media and events.

Astronomines came into existence in 2016, founded by engineering students from diverse majors at École Nationale Supérieure des Mines de Rabat in Rabat, Morocco.
